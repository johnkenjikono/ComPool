Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

❓ What is ComPool?

ComPool is the easiest way to pool money, invite friends, and compete in a fun and transparent way. Whether you're managing a shared fund, setting up a friendly competition, or tracking group contributions, we've got you covered!

❓ How does ComPool work?

1️⃣ Create a Pool – Start a shared fund for any purpose.

2️⃣ Invite Friends – Easily add participants and track contributions.

3️⃣ Contribute & Compete – Set rules, pool money, and reward winners automatically.

❓ Is ComPool free to use?

Yes! ComPool is free for Android, iPhone, and Web. We also offer premium features for those who want enhanced tracking and automation.
